About US

Vertrauen Management LTD is a private wealth manager. We offer a range of innovative investment products for both individual and institutional investors. Our experience cuts across sectors and asset classes.

We work to understand your specific needs, offering a range of investment products and services across various asset classes and sectors. From mutual funds to liquidity management, we work with individual and institutional investors to provide a strategy best suited to your investment goals and portfolios.

OUR SERVICES

Mutual Funds

Mutual funds provide you with access to investment markets by pooling your money with the money of several other individuals with similar investment goals.

Portfolio Management

Based on your investment objectives, we help to manage your wealth in a way that consistently surpasses the market.

Liquidity Management

Centered on your specific personal and business needs, we offer short term investment management solutions.

Structured Products

We provide pre-packaged investment solutions, designed to facilitate highly customized risk-return objectives.
